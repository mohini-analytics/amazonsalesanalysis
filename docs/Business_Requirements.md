
<b> Business Requirements Document (BRD)</b> 

<b>  Business Objective: 
Analyse Amazon UK product data to identify trends in category performance, pricing, reviews, and ratings to improve product quality, customer satisfaction, and commercial outcomes.

<b>  Scope </b> 
- Dataset: 600K+ Amazon UK product records  
- Tools: SQL (BigQuery), R, Power BI  
- Focus areas: category performance, pricing strategy, customer perception

<b>  Stakeholders </b> 
- Category Managers  
- Pricing & Commercial Teams  
- Operations & Quality Teams  
- Senior Management

<b>  Key Business Questions </b> 
- Which categories perform strongly in demand but poorly in customer satisfaction?
- How does pricing influence ratings and review volume?
- Where should quality improvement or pricing intervention be prioritised?

<b>  Success Criteria </b> 
- Clear identification of high- and low-performing categories
- Actionable insights supported by data
- Outputs usable in dashboards and decision-making
